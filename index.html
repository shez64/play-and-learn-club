We Welcome You To The Play & Learn Club!

The Play and Learn Club in Ashburn, VA is a home-based, fun, and safe environment in which children are able to grow strong physically and develop well emoionally, all while building long-lasting relationships!

We have been in business for about 14 years and have had the pleasure of teaching dozens of children their basic essentials!

We work with children aged 3 months all the way until the age of 5.

Our instructors work with you and your children to ensure they are getting the resources they need to learn and enjoy their time here.
